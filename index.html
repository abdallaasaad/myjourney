The Product

Dwelli eliminates roommate payment friction and brings clarity to how shared apartments run — financially and socially.

Problem

  • Shared apartments run on awkward splits, missed payments, and manual bill tracking.
  • Roommates argue over who owes what — rent, utilities, groceries, and one-off expenses.
  • Landlords lack visibility into how shared units actually operate financially.

Solution

  • A Tenant OS — a financial and social operating system for shared apartments.
  • Automates shared payments, bill splitting, and apartment financial management.
  • A shared digital apartment card connects roommates, landlords, bills, utilities, contracts, and payments.

Why investors care

Shared living is growing, but financial friction in co-living remains unsolved at the OS layer. Dwelli is designed to become the trusted infrastructure for roommate payments and apartment finances — recurring usage, clear monetization, and expansion across every shared unit.

Our Journey

From lived experience in shared housing to building the operating system it always needed.

Discovery
We observed a simple pattern: shared apartments have no financial OS — every roommate group reinvents bill splits, payment chasing, and who owes what.
Validation
We spoke with roommates and landlords and mapped where friction actually breaks trust: rent splits, utilities, contracts, and the invisible work of keeping a shared unit running.
Building
We are building Dwelli as the smart layer between people and payments — automation, shared payments, and a digital apartment card that keeps everyone aligned.
De-risking
The core differentiator is owning the apartment's financial layer — less chasing, clearer accountability, and smoother coordination between roommates and landlords.
Now
We are preparing pilots and building partnerships. If you are an angel investor or advisor who values practical execution in fintech × proptech — we would love to talk.
