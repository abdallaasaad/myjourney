StudentKey

Problem

  • Student apartments have frequent turnover — every year the process repeats.
  • Landlords lose time and money: vacancies, scheduling, screening, paperwork.
  • Payment risk adds stress — especially with new tenants each cycle.

Solution

  • Student‑focused listings near campuses, built for fast, relevant matching.
  • We reduce landlord workload around turnover and the rental process.
  • Optional rent payment assurance through a financing partner for peace of mind.

Why investors care

Student housing is a recurring market with predictable annual demand. StudentKey is designed to become the trusted layer between landlords and student tenants: relevant supply, smoother leasing, and a path to financial products that reduce risk.

My journey with StudentKey

Discovery
I started by observing a simple pattern: in student rentals, turnover is guaranteed. The real pain is everything turnover causes — vacancy gaps, time loss, and stress.
Validation
I spoke with students and landlords and mapped the actual decision flow: where students search, what “relevant” means near a campus, and what landlords hate repeating every year.
Building
I built an MVP that focuses only on student‑relevant apartments and a workflow that reduces friction for both sides. The product is designed to scale with clear ops and trust.
De‑risking
The core differentiator is taking responsibility for the painful parts of turnover — and enabling reliable payments by connecting to a financing partner that can guarantee rent flows.
Now
I’m preparing pilots, onboarding supply, and building partnerships. If you’re an angel investor or advisor who likes practical execution and clear markets — I’d love to talk.
